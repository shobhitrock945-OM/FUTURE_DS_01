Top 7 Actionable Recommendations

  1. Focus on cellular channel — converts at 14.7% vs telephone's ~6.5%. Reallocate budget accordingly.
  2. Train agents to keep calls past 3 minutes — calls over 3 min are 4× more likely to convert.
  3. Prioritize warm leads — prospects with prior successful outcomes convert at 65%+.
  4. Cap contacts at 3 per prospect — conversion drops sharply after 3 calls; further calls waste resources.
  5. Schedule campaigns in March, September, October — these months consistently show the highest conversion rates.
  6. Target student and retired segments — these job categories have the highest conversion rates and respond well to tailored offers.
  7. Build re-engagement pipelines — 50% of prospects engage (3+ min call) but don't convert; follow-up strategies can recapture them.
